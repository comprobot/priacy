TV Channel list

Press the button "channel list" from left hand upside


Select the channel from Remote TV channel list:

You can select categories and countries to filter out the TV channels as you want. In addition, you can search for the TV channels based on a keyword.




Add your favourite TV channel list


After getting the TV channel list , you can press the button "Add Favourite channels" to add the channel to your favourite channel lists.




Add your M3U file and insert to your favourite TV channels

Press the button "Add you M3u file" from left hand upside
